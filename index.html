about me

I have loved computers for as long as I can remember, from when I built my first PC at age fourteen to when I got my first virus on my laptop at age eight and had to get rid of it myself so my dad didn't tell me off! Computers have held a special place in my heart! I enjoy not only working on the hardware of computers but also the software! I started developing programs in Scratch at an early age, I'd guess around 9, then moved on to Python, C, Javascript, and many more. On the hardware side, I loved making and tinkering with the PCs I had made. I have worked on liquid-cooled PCs, to now more technical devices like servers, routers, and switches. This allows me to run my home server which runs as not only a file server for my local network but outside my LAN too, in a sense creating my own cloud storage for my friends. To learn more about these projects and my experience, click the options above.

I also do public speaking about mental health and my experience. This ties into the job I currently work at. I have spoken in front of many professionals and students to help them better understand mental health and what it feels like to be in the situation as I am now on the other side. This is more of a passion project as I love to help others, you can see my work at www.betterfutureuk.com
